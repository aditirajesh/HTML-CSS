ICONIC WORLDS

IN MARIO KART 🌎

🚗💨 From lush meadows to haunted mansions and cosmic highways, explore the unforgettable tracks that make every Mario Kart race a wild adventure!

  • The heart of Mario’s adventures! 🍄 A colorful land full of Toads, green pipes, and surprise blocks waiting to be hit. It’s where all the magic begins!Most famously featured in Super Mario Bros. (1985), this world is the original stage of Mario’s heroic journey and the face of Nintendo history.

  • Enter… if you dare! 🐢🔥 Full of lava pits, fireballs, and traps, Bowser’s Castle is the ultimate villain lair. It's scary — but thrilling!A final boss staple — this fiery fortress has appeared in nearly every Mario game and is known for its epic music and lava-filled chaos.

  • A super cute island filled with eggs, flutter jumps, and friendly dinosaurs! 🦖 Yoshi’s Island is colorful, cozy, and always a blast to explore.First starred in Super Mario World 2: Yoshi’s Island (SNES), and became so beloved that it launched its own spin-off series!

  • Sunshine, scooters, and seaside streets! ☀️🛵 Delfino Square is the perfect tropical town where the sun never stops shining. A fan-favorite from Mario Kart DS, and part of the sunny, vibrant Isle Delfino setting from Super Mario Sunshine.

  • Royal halls, stained-glass windows, and secret stars! 👑 Peach’s Castle is both a home and a hub of magical mysteries.First explorable in Super Mario 64, it became iconic for its hidden stars, secret walls, and endless nostalgia.

  • The most dazzling (and dangerous!) track in the galaxy! 🌈🚀 Zoom through stars and space with no rails to stop you — don’t fall! Famous for its difficulty and beauty, it appears in every Mario Kart game and is beloved (and feared!) by racers everywhere

  • Lights, mud, and mischief! 💥 Wario’s Stadium is wild, wacky, and full of jumps and bumps — just like Wario himself. Known for wild jumps and explosive hazards, this track is a chaotic highlight in Mario Kart 64 and Mario Kart DS.

  • Shopping has never been this fun! 🛍️ Coconut Mall is packed with escalators, cars, and chaos — race your friends through a tropical mall! Became a meme legend in Mario Kart Wii — and fans were thrilled when it returned in Mario Kart 8 Deluxe Booster Pack.

  • Moo-ve over! 🐄🌾 This peaceful farm is filled with rolling hills, cute cows, and fresh country air. Just don’t hit a Moo Moo! This charming countryside track from Mario Kart Wii is a low-stress favorite — until the cows get in your way!

  • Ghosts, secret doors, and spooky fun! 👻🏰 Twisted Mansion is a haunted house filled with mysteries and magical thrills. A haunted highlight in Mario Kart 8, often praised for its eerie atmosphere and twisting, ghostly décor.
